Family Constellation

Family Constellation is a therapeutic approach developed by Bert Hellinger, a German psychotherapist, which uses the creation of "living sculptures" to reconstruct the family tree and identify blockages in the flow of love within any generation or family member. This method allows you to reveal and resolve personal difficulties and relationship problems that often originate from disorders in family systems.

A Path of Healing and Growth

These disorders arise when, without realizing it, we adopt the destiny of someone in our family — whether a living member or a deceased member — as if it were our own. This phenomenon causes us to repeat the fate of relatives who were excluded, forgotten or unrecognized, occupying the place that should have been theirs.
Juntamente com uma colega, busco oferecer uma base sólida para que o processo de constelação ocorra da melhor maneira possível. O feedback que recebo após cada constelação reforça que estamos realizando um bom trabalho e proporcionando um ambiente seguro. Minha abordagem é apoiada por referências de autores renomados como Bert Hellinger, Els Thissen, Franz Ruppert e Els van Steyn.

Emotional Support Through Family Constellations
I really wanted to conduct constellations because it is such a wonderful healing method that provides clarity and growth for so many people. I wanted and can support this process in the constellation with my quality and life experience. Which helps me, as my son described it: 'you have a big heart'.

In addition to my professional development, for many years I have been dedicating myself to spiritual growth and the practice of living in the present. I continue to learn to accept circumstances as they are, seeking to be in harmony with myself and releasing what does not serve me personally. As part of this journey, I also practice Biodanza, sing in a choir and dedicate myself to painting as a hobby.

Furthermore, I continue to seek and foster connections between people, something I have always valued in my work. Currently, I contribute to this mission in a camping association and a neighborhood association. At the campsite, I also offer, together with an experienced colleague, relaxing massages, where I realize how essential respectful touch is for many.
