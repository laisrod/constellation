What are the benefits of family constellation?

Our encounter
with revelation and healing.

01.

Resolution of emotional problems

Facilitates the resolution of emotional problems by revealing and addressing hidden dynamics and conflicts in the family system. By understanding these influences, you can release emotional blocks, promoting healing and internal balance. It is a powerful tool for overcoming trauma and improving your emotional well-being.

02.

Connection with your life purpose

Enhances connection with your life purpose by unraveling inherited patterns that may be blocking the path to self-realization. By releasing these influences, you can align more deeply with your true mission and meaning in life, promoting greater balance and satisfaction.

03.

Reestablish good relationships with relatives

This process facilitates the healing of emotional wounds, promotes forgiveness and strengthens mutual understanding, allowing family bonds to become healthier and more harmonious.

What are the 3 orders of Love, described by Bert Hellinger?

1st Law of Love
2st Law of Love
3st Law of Love

The Law of Hierarchy

The first order emphasizes our deep need for a sense of belonging, along with the need for any living system to honor and integrate all of its parts.

The Law of Belonging

The second order highlights the importance of understanding and honoring the hierarchy or order of arrival within living systems.

The Law of Equilibrium of Exchanges

The third order illuminates the reciprocity and balance that allows life to flow and life and love to thrive when there is a healthy exchange of give and take.
